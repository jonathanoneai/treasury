Round Sea Shell Encrusted Pink Flower Motif Decorative Box with Lid

About the Item

A pretty coastal-inspired round decorative box encrusted with a sea shell motif. This box is round and features an attached lid. The lid is decorated with an array of seashells and topped with pink painted shells arranged in a flower motif. The lid opens to reveal a red velvet lining.
