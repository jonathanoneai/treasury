ZODIAC x WATCHES.COM COLLAB
WHERE TIME MEETS SPACE

Watches.com is known for carrying the most unique, eye-catching
watches online and has been a long-time retailer of Zodiac. Back in 2019,
Zodiac approached us to create a one-of-a-kind Super Sea Wolf and we
designed something thats out of this world - the Zodiac Super Sea Wolf Meteorite.

Throughout its history, Zodiac has dedicated itself to the precision and
craftsmanship associated with Swiss timekeeping, all while exploring new
materials and pushing boundaries to create dynamic timepieces.

This Limited Edition Super Sea Wolf Meteorite is Zodiacs first exploration
into the elemental nature of time and space with a material that was
literally forged across the universe.

The meteorite used on the dial was crafted from a genuine Muonionalusta
Meteorite that spent billions of years traveling through the universe until
chance pulled it into Earths orbit and it landed in the Norrbotten region
of Sweden approximately 800,000 years ago. When cut, the metal from
the meteorite reveals one-of-a-kind patterns of iron and nickel that look
distinctly extraterrestrial and uniquely modern.

Featuring super-luminova hands and markers for unparalleled visibility in
all light conditions and a space gray stainless steel DLC case for ultimate
durability, this limited edition iteration highlights the iconic Super Sea Wolf
design with a nod towards the wonder of outer space. Complemented by
a brushed five-link space gray stainless steel butterfly-clasp bracelet, the
Super Sea Wolf Meteorite case holds a Swiss-made STP 1-11 automatic
movement and is limited to only 182 units.
