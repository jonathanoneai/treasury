Men's Hemp and Organic Cotton Fleece Sweatpants - Shorter/Smaller Version

Our new hemp and organic cotton hemp sweatpants are made from the same heavy fleece fabric as our other sweatpants. However, they have a slimmer and shorter cut than our previous "tall" version. They also now have two back pockets instead of one. They are still comfortable, durable, and sustainable. Comfy enough to wear lounging around the house while being stylish enough to wear out.

Hemp Clothing Benefits:
-Hemp fiber is lightweight and water absorbent
-Durable, long-lasting, and strong with 3-4 times the strength of cotton
-UV and mold resistant which is excellent for outdoor wear
-Hemp is porous which allows it to breathe to keep you cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter
-Absorbs moisture quickly and resists growth of mildew and bacteria
-Breathes excellently, preventing odors
-Retains color
-Soft and comfortable and can be blended with organic cotton and other fibers
-Hemp clothing is soft on skin and becomes softer each wear
-Easy care with excellent durability
-Earth friendly - Chemical and pesticide free and uses very little water to grow

Fabric Care: Machine Washable

Hemp and cotton are both all-natural fibers. Therefore, special care is recommended when
washing these fabrics. Wash in cold to warm water. Remove from the washing machine, stretch out
especially legs and sleeves. Then lie flat or hang dry. If the product is a little long, then you may want
to place in the drier for a few minutes for slight shrinkage. If clothing was placed in the drier and
undesired shrinkage occurred, re-wash and follow instructions above.

Please note: We include a free sample of one of our hemp oil soaps in each order. If you prefer not to receive a sample, please let us know at checkout in the note to seller section.
