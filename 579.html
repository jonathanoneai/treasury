Provenance

Pharrell Williams

Product Note

Following the debut of his black Timberland boots in 2011, Pharrell debuted a red version in 2014. These boots served as both another Chanel collaboration and a political statement. Featured on the boots are sayings such as "Don't Same The World," "Change," "Your Time," and "Human Connection" alongside a Chanel logo painted on the right toe. The red Timberland 6-inch waterproof black nubuck boot features hand-painted motifs and was worn by Pharrell when he was presented with a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.

These boots are in used condition.
