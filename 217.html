Shop Pre-owned Vintage Designer Accessories Online at WGACA

Complement your ensembles with the final flourish of luxury accessories and discover pieces that reveal the grace and timelessness of iconic designer brands.

Wonder through a world of uncommon opulence paired with quality as you explore our vintage designer accessories. The collection ranges from essential counterparts, including hats, sunglasses, wallets and belts, to enviable items like luxury home décor accessories to unique sports finds and classy cold weather accompaniments.

Designer sunglasses are as desirable as shades come; find them by fashion houses that are an embodiment to luxury and elegance, including iconic Chanel sunglasses and the ever-so rare Chrome Hearts sunglasses.

Much like shades, luxury hats lend utter flair and power to your style. Explore a range of Dior hats and Gucci hats, including high-end baseball caps, fedoras and beanies.

Bag charms and scarves are the perfect handbag accessory. With their versatility, you can add a personalized look when you complement your bag with a luxury bag charm or wrap an elegant scarf around the handles. WGACA houses a range including fur Louis Vuitton scarves, rare Hermès silk scarves and Chanel scarves.

You can also find a coveted selection of designer belts and suspenders to embellish your waist with profound luxury. Discover Monogram Louis Vuitton belts and chain Chanel belts or get the best of both worlds with designer belt bags.

WGACA proposes an extensive curation of sought-after designer men’s accessories in addition. Build a capsule collection of must-have items with men’s designer wallets and cardholders by luxury fashion houses like Gucci and Louis Vuitton.

For luxury gifts for loved ones or yourself, take your pick from pristine finds to buy once and keep forever, only at WGACA.
