Lee Radziwill Inspired Block Print Down Flower Motif Pillow in Pink, India

About the Item

Bring a taste of the orient to any space with one of our custom-created Indian block print pillows. Each pillow is made of soft cotton and hand block printed by our artisans in India. Set on a bright coral pink background, blue blocked flowers flank both the front and back. A down-fill insert is included with each pillow. The corners are knife-edge, with a hidden zip on the bottom. This limited edition is inspired by the home of Lee Radziwill at 4 Buckingham Place. Radziwill hired Italian designer Renzo Mongiardino who swathed the space with various hues of block print fabric both on the walls, devan, and lampshades. Truly a statement room! Dimensions: 16" Wide 16" Tall 6" Deep.
