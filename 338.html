18k Bespoke 3.25 Carat Tanzanite Marquise Ring

This tantalizing tanzanite ring was sourced as a vintage setting, missing its stone. We have added a large, bright periwinkle tanzanite, set east-west, for a bold new look.

Tanzanite is nearly 1000 times rarer than diamonds, sourced only from a single mine in Tanzania, hence the name. The value of this stone continues to increase as it becomes rarer, and even with investment opportunities aside- its gorgeous color is unparalleled! 

Though currently a small size 4, it can be sized up or down for an additional fee. 6.2 grams and with the coolest geometric shape, it sits flush to the finger and has knife-edge shoulders.
