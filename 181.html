      WHAT IS MOSS AGATE? 

      Moss agate is a semi-precious stone known for its green-colored filaments inside of the stone which resemble moss. Each stone is different and will vary in appearance, which makes the stone in each ring extremely unique! A specific look in a stone cannot be replicated, as these are not lab-created stones, so please realize that the stone used in your ring will not look the same as the stone in the listing photos.

      Please note that due to the natural material of moss agate, the color of the "white space" in the stone itself can also vary greatly - anywhere from murky and cream-colored to almost being clear and see-through. We cannot control the outcome of these stones, and we do not offer free stone replacements due to the aesthetic and natural variation that will occur.

      **Moss agates can be difficult to source, please be aware that we cannot guarantee an exact ship date and it may take longer than our standard 8 week production time. If you are on a time constraint and would like to confirm shipping dates prior to purchasing please send us an email!

      CARE GUIDELINES

      Men’s/Unisex Rings

      Because many of our rings have natural materials in them (such as wood, antler, etc.) they do require more care than a traditional solid gold wedding band. We recommend you remove your ring for:

      - Any water exposure (including handwashing)
      - Using household chemicals, hand sanitizer, or any other harsh chemicals
      - Any manual labor
      - Anything that might incur impact damage such as hitting your ring on hard surfaces or dropping it

      Engagement Rings

      - Remove your ring for any manual labor (especially anything that includes gripping items tightly such as furniture, free weights, etc.)
      - Remove your ring when using cosmetics, household chemicals, hand sanitizer, or any other harsh chemicals

      Click here to view our full care guidelines for all of our rings.
