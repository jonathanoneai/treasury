  • DESCRIPTION

    Making the everyday extraordinary has long been part of the Anya Hindmarch story. These sequinned evening tote bags are a luxurious, beautifully-crafted take on our favourite household brands and create a whimsical evening bag option.

    Item number: 5050925143936

  • SPECIFICATION & MATERIALS

    Dimensions: D 2cm W 17cm H 30cm

    Satin, Sequins, crystals

    Leather patch embossed with Anya Hindmarch logo

    The embellishment on this product is very delicate, so please handle with care. We would recommend that care be taken when wearing this product, as it could cause snagging or marking when worn with delicate fabrics. Please avoid contact with liquids as they could remove the finish of the product. This product is not suitable for children under 36 months due to small parts. This piece is handmade and so each one is unique. You may notice the colour and finish varies.
