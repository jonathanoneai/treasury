    ELEGANCE WITH A WOW AFFECT

    Trade your comfort zone for a great adventure! If you want to change the world, the first thing you need is positive energy and the courage to try something new. The Chronograph Blue Orange has plenty of both: the combination of electric blue with intense orange, plus glow in the dark Superluminova and a stop function that is accurate to the tenth of a second make the sporty, elegant design almost crackle with energy. 

    AWARD-WINNING, RADIANT DESIGN

    Superluminova-coated elements and bright orange highlights make this award-winning design shine.

    TIMER FUNCTION AND DATE DISPLAY

    The Chronograph is precise to 1/10th of a second, with a date window that keeps an eye on the big picture.

    QUALITY IN EVERY DETAIL

    A movement from Swiss manufacturer Ronda, surgical steel, and sapphire-hardened glass. Made to last.

    SUPPLE MESH STRAP

    The stainless steel strap is comfortable, durable and easy to change thanks to the quick-release mechanism.

    ‘MADE IN GERMANY’ QUALITY

    Designed in Berlin and crafted in Germany in line with the highest quality and sustainability standards.

    The Chronograph Blue Orange in detail

    EVERYTHING BUT MAINSTREAM

    Playing it safe? That's one way to do it but it's more fun to throw all conventious out the window and do things your own way. After all, individuality doesn't go by the book. And the design of the Chronograph Blue Orange goes it's own way too: the Extroverted blue-orange mix puts urban understatement in a whole new light. It features a unique interplay of light and dark. In addition, clear lines emphasize corners and edges. All this makes the watch a declaration of love for the extraordinary!

    STYLE IN FULL TEMPO

    Keeping an eye on the present. Ready to stop and start time. Always ahead of the moment. Looking ahead requires some courage as well. Sometimes the happiest memories come from the boldest adventures. Bright orange coexisting with intense blue and clear-cut lines bouncing off smooth rounded shapes. And that is how we have given that extra "oomph" to this Chronograph's urban elegance.

    STOP AND GO

    The Startech Caliber 3540.D from Swiss manufacturer Ronda is the driving force behind the watch, allowing time to be accurately measured to the tenth of a second. It’s reliable, durable, and offers high functionality. With just two push buttons, you can measure time to your heart's content. How long does it take to get from A to B, or vice versa? What’s the fastest route to achieving your dreams? Keep track of the best times with just the push of a button.

    POWER IN DETAIL

    A dark shimmering metallic tone and bursts of intense orange create an explosive style. A purist baseline and a whole world to discover, such as the crown shaped as the famous world clock at Alexanderplatz or the typography of Berlin’s street signs. The award-winning design of the Chronograph in blue orange makes the best impression, time and time again.

    STRONG STEEL, SMOOTH FEEL

    The strap is made from interwoven strands of 316L surgical stainless steel finished in a way that’s extremely robust, anti-allergenic and never rusts. Length can be adjusted for an optimal fit. And if you feel it's time for a new look, the strap can be changed easily thanks to the quick-release mechanism.

    QUALITY IS OUR STANDARD

    All our watches are designed in Berlin and manufactured in Germany together with hand-picked partners and equally carefully selected materials. Because we never make compromises, we guarantee a watch that’s built to last with quality that’s anything but standard. Our philosophy? Longevity means sustainability!
