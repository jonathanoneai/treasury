Serpentine Sofa by Vladimir Kagan in Navy Linen, Model 150BS

About

A stunning and rare Serpentine sofa, Model 150BS designed by architect and furniture designer Vladimir Kagan. It features a distinctive curved frame that gives this piece a delightful sensual design, assuring a striking proportioned profile view from all angles. It has been completely restored, down to its frame with all insides fully updated (springs, foam, straps). Re-upholstered in navy blue linen by ROMO. Supports are ebonised walnut. Other designers working in the organic style include Gio Ponti, Jean Royere, Paul Frankl, George Nakashima, and Isamu Noguchi.
