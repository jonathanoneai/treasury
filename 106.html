    Modern design

    Designed, conceived, and assembled in France, the Initial Open Heart rose gold gives to its automatic movement the most beautiful function. Its sandblasted ivory open-heart dial reveals its beautiful and complex mechanism, in addition to displaying the hours, minutes and seconds, as well as the 24h-counter. This automatic men's watch combines a modern elegance with a layed-back refinement. With its high-quality components, its mastered sobriety and its interchangeable straps, this watch will accompany you for as long as you desire. This men's watch, powered by the energy of its carrier, has a transparent sapphire caseback allowing you to admire the caliber with its rotor signed Charlie Paris.

    CASE

    • Polished stainless steel 316L case
    • 40mm diameter
    • 45.5mm horn to horn length
    • 11.9mm thickness
    • Sapphire glass, ultra-resistant, anti-reflective
    • Transparent sapphire crystal caseback
    • Waterproof 3ATM

    DIAL AND HANDS

    • Sandblasted dial
    • Seconds at 4 o'clock
    • 24-hour counter at 9 o'clock
    • Applique hour index
    • Spear-shaped hour and minute hands
    • External display for the time marks

    MOVEMENT

    • Automatic movement Citizen Miyota Caliber 82S7
    • Display hour, minute, small second, 24h and open heart
    • -20 +40s per day
    • 21 rubies
    • Power reserve of 40 hours

    STRAP

    • Leather bracelet
    • Interchangeable without any tools
    • 20mm width
    • 110 / 80mm length
    • Pin buckle

    MADE IN FRANCE

    Assembled in France with care

    It is important to us to act locally, which is why our watches are assembled by hand in France by professional and experienced watchmakers.

    QUALITY

    sapphire glass

    The watch is equipped with a sapphire glass which is a highly resistant component offering an excellent readability of the dial. This material is the strongest after the diamond, and is used in the high-end watchmaking industry for its anti-scratch properties.

    QUALITY

    316L steel case

    The case is made of 316L steel, an alloy known for its durability and also its high resistance to corrosion. From an aesthetic point of view, 316L steel is a stainless steel whose shine lasts over time and does not tarnish.

    SUSTAINABILITY

    ECO-FRIENDLY PACKAGING

    Our packagings are manufactured by an ISO4001 certified partner, which ensures efficient and environmental management of the company. They are designed from recycled cardboard, ABS which is a recyclable polymer and with solvent-free glue. The paper we use is FSC certified.

    Interchangeable strap

    The perfect choice for a wrist in search of versatility. 20% automatic discount on the second strap. All our watches are offered with straps equipped with so-called "flash" pumps. Thanks to them, you can change your strap in just a few seconds and without any tools.
