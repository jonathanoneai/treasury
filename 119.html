                              Product Care
                              • Our bags are hand crafted using genuine leather. Like all natural materials, leather reacts to the atmosphere.
                                Humidity, rain and sunlight can damage leather.
                                Should a slight white coating appear on the surface of the leather, it can easily be removed using a dry cloth.
                                Should the leather get wet, please absorb the water immediately with a dry cloth.
                                Overexposure to sunlight can stain or alter the color of the leather.
